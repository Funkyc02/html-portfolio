Caleb's List of Favorite Movies

My Top 3

Remember the Titans

Very iconic movie for me and my family. Feel good, inspiring, and funny movie about a high school football team during the strenuous time of integration in schools.

The Dark Knight

An objectively perfect movie with an amazing villain. Heath Ledger as the Joker is phenomenal. Batman's theme gives me chills. Christoper Nolan FTW.

Interstellar

I cry everytime I watch this movie, it is a masterpiece. Christopher Nolan once again delivers in absolute genius, Hans Zimmer crafts what is arguably the greatest soundtrack of all-time.
